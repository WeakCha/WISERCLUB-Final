Welcome to WISERCLUB 2019-2020 Final

Preface

You have got two csv files, named data.csv and holiday.csv. Try to use Python or SQL to complete the tasks with the aid of these two datasets. Please keep in mind that Data Science or Business Analytics is to use data and models to find hidden information.

There will be some hints for each small task, most of them are listed here, but a few of them are not shown. This is because the corresponding problems require close analysis and observation. The days for them to be shown on the website are marked with bold words, but please try to find answers by yourself before that time.

This project consists of three parts: Explorative Data Analysis, Data Preprocessing and Model Training and Prediction. The final decision about whether one will be admitted in WISERCLUB or not DOES NOT solely depend on the project.

Now, you could begin your exploration in the ocean of data.

Notes and other Requirements

  1. For all final takers, be responsible for your work!

  2. For all students in WISERCLUB Undergraduate Department ONLY , you are required to complete tasks from the beginning to Part 2-1 and you are recommended to complete all tasks because the whole project is worth to be a big project for Data Science, Business Analytics and related areas.

  3. For all students in WISERCLUB Undergraduate Department ONLY , you could discuss with each other as a group, but your codes should not be the same. Plagiarism will lead to an automatic denial for your admission.

  4. Try not overwrite either of your files. This is very dangerous in real work. The number of times you ask for the files of the original datasets will have an impact on the final evaluation of your work.
